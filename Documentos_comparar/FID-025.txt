Empathy in artificial intelligence (AI) aims to enhance human-computer interactions by enabling AI to understand and respond to human emotions. This paper examines techniques such as natural language processing and affective computing used to create empathetic AI systems. Applications in mental health, customer service, and personalized education are discussed, showcasing the potential benefits of empathetic AI. The study also addresses ethical concerns, including emotional manipulation and privacy issues, emphasizing the need for ethical guidelines and unbiased algorithms in developing empathetic AI.
